Top 3 Favorite Movies/Series



Squid Game

Squid game is a popular Korean survival thriller on Netflix where financially desperate individuals
compete in deadly versions of children's games for a massive cash prize, exploring themes of economic
inequality, morality, and so on. The whole concept of the show really drew my attention, making me
interested in all 3 series.

Characters

Seong Gi-hun (Player 456)
Kang No-eul (Guard 011)
Cho Hyun-ju (Player 120)



Train to Busan

Train to Busan is a Korean zombie film about passengers on a bullet train from Seoul to Busan who
must fight for their survival when a virus turns people into zombies during their journey. All
throughout the movie, there were thrilling scenes and action everywhere but what hit me the hardest
was the ending I didn't expect.

Characters

Seok-Woo
Sang-Hwa
Sung-Gyeong



Alice in Borderland

Similar to Squid Game, Alice in Borderland showcases a teenage gamer and his two friends who find
themselves in a parallel Tokyo, where they're forced to compete in a series of games to survive. These
games consist of 4 categories that require you to either utilize your mind, heart, physical fitness,
or ability to work as a group. Though there were devastating deaths, the whole series was interesting
and entertaining.

Characters

Hikari Kuina
Rizuna Ann
Shuntaro Chishiya
